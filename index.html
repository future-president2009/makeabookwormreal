The Competition

Submit a video about the book you read by July 31st!
Upload a video of yourself on YouTube
Make sure to tag #makeyourselfabookwormchallenge in the description box.

Ideas for your video:

Enact a scene in the book.

Write a short review of the book.

Draw your favorite scene from the book.

All kids have three weeks to read the books. July 31st will be the last date to submit the videos, and then the judging will begin. If your child doesn't want to make a video, you can share their article/drawing via Twitter. There will be three winners in each grade level.
